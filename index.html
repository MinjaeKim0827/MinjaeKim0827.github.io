About Me

Hello! I'm Minjae Kim, an undergraduate student in physics at KAIST. My research focus lies in astrophysics, particularly in understanding the physical processes that govern the structure and evolution of the universe.

Currently, I am conducting research in the OBSCAL group at KAIST, studying cosmology and galaxy evolution using cosmological simulations. I plan to graduate in the spring of 2027 and continue my academic journey by pursuing graduate studies in astrophysics.
